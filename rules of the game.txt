Rules of the game:

You choose one of the 3 options: stone, scissors or paper.
If you want to select "stone", enter the number 1, if you select "scissors" enter number 2,
if your choice is "paper", enter number 3.
Further choice makes the computer and results are compared.

The stone defeats the scissors (the stone blunts or breaks the scissors).
Paper wins the stone (paper covers the stone).
Scissors win paper (scissors cut paper).
